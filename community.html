CommunityDev.

Immunisation Camp at Park School

Max India foundation and Sopra Steria organised an immunization camp for Typhoid on 16th November 2018 at Park school. Around 100 students got benefits who were between 5 to 16 years of age.

Health Camps at Park School

Dental check up camp was organized by Round Table Group, Noida for the junior students in the Park school on 1st october 2017.

Immunization camp by Sopra Steria, Noida in collaboration with Max Foundation was organized on 10th October and 8th November 2017. 300 children (age group 5-16 year) were vaccinated for typhoid.

Movie screening in April 2017

On 13 April 2017 145 students of class 6th to 12th and 39 parents along with 6 teachers went to watch a movie Poorna at Spice Mall, Noida. The movie is about Poorna Malavath the youngest girl in the World from Telangana, India, who conquered Mount Everest at the age of 13. Our heartfelt thanks to Ms. Namita Gautam who sponsored the movie.

Solar Lamp installation in Basti

Steria Noida Brings MORE Light to GSV Basti with Street Light. Installation work in progress.

EYE Camp in Basti in May and July 2017

An Eye Camp was organized at Basti by Steria India. Noida on 24th May and 24th July, 2017. Approximately 120 residents and their children of basti were benefited by t his camp. They were given medicines and eye drops.

Session on Road Safety for students in July 2017

IL&FS conducted a session on Road Safety for primary students (3rd to 5th classes) on 5th July 2017. A film was also screened at Miniland School to raise awareness about Road Safety.
