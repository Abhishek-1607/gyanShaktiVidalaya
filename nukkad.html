NukkadNatak

Nukkad Natak by Theater Team 2018

This year the theme of our Nukkad Natak was “Say no to plastics”. It was directed by Mr. Nadeem Khan. To convey this message the group of 11 students performed it at various places such as Indian Oil Corporation Noida and Lodhi Road Branch, Navyug School and Kendriya Vidyalaya in Delhi.

Nukkad Natak by Theater Team

In order to promote awareness of the most important issue of the day ‘Cleanliness’, GSV trained 11 students (age group 10-16 years) to perform Nukkad Natak “Swachhata ki Soch”. They were invited by various organizations which includes:

  1. To inspire the socially economically backward children into the mainstream by primarily supporting their educational needs along with social, health and hygiene care.
  2. To enable them to become responsible, capable & employable citizens with pride and sense of belonging to their community and the nation.
