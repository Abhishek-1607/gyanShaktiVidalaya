Donate

With the overwhelming and unconditional support from the concerned citizens, we continue to render our humble service to these most-deserving children.


Being a non-profit set up we rely heavily on private donations. Only 3% of our budget goes into administration, the remainder goes entirely to direct program expenditure. Salaries are modest, our home is our office and we stretch our funds to maximize their impact.


Put simply, if you could possibly manage to support our initiative, you will transform the lives of several children in desperate need.

Our MajorExpense

  • Salaries for teachers
  • Co-curricular activities like Field Trips
  • Cultural and Art workshops
  • Maintenance of Community school's infrastructure
  • School uniforms, shoes, bags etc
  • Services of behavioral counselors

Donatein Kind
